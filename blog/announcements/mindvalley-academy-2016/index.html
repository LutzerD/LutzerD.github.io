4 New Changes To Your Mindvalley Academy Experience In 2016

Vishen Lakhiani — December 28, 2015

I just want to thank you for your incredible support and we’re extremely grateful to have you as a Mindvalley member.

As we go into 2016, we made some changes designed to make your experience with the Mindvalley Academy even more joyful, personalized, and transformational:

Elula

To improve your learning experience, we’ve spent the last 18 months investing in building a new learning platform.

Our aim was to make the courses on Mindvalley Academy easy to consume, beautiful to experience, and be available online and offline. We have already rolled out our first course on the new platform — Consciousness Engineering — and more and more of our programs will move onto it early in the year.

You will also be able to download programs to your phone, speed up the audio, and much more.

World class authors

Every year, we have been trying to bring you the biggest names in the personal growth. This year, you’ve heard from the likes of Marissa Peer and Michael Beckwith.

Next year, look forward to hearing from nutrition experts like J.J Virgin, Psalm Isadora, and Andrew Holocek.

Health and Diet expert JJ Virgin

Author and Spiritual Teacher, Andrew Holocek

Sex and Sensuality Coach, Psalm Isadora

Tribelearn

We’ve found that the best way to learn is to learn as a group.

So, we decided to invest in peer-to-peer learning software, which lets you learn from other members. With every course you buy, we want you to be part of a community of people going through the same material, sharing your experiences, challenges, and revelations along the way…

It’s called Tribelearn and is already available on programs like Consciousness Engineering and Soulvana Circle and is rolling out soon for the rest of our programs.

Channels for your interests

Rather than just having one Mindvalley Academy that covers all aspects of personal growth, we’re establishing separate interest groups.

For example, if you’re only interested in spirituality and energy work, sign up for Soulvana Circle and you’ll only be notified of Masterclasses that fit that category. If you only care about Yoga, join upcoming yoga channel, Zenward. We’ll also be launching work and productivity.

Also, look forward to more academies launching in niches like impact, work, and productivity.

As you can tell, the next 12 months are going to be very exciting, and we hope to bring you more and more innovations to truly revolutionize your learning experience with us.

So once again, thank you for your support as a Mindvalley member, have a wonderful New Year, and here’s hoping 2016 turns into an extraordinary year for you.
